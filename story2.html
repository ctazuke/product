Franken Prototype

I made a franken prototype by paper of grocery bag and scotch tape. Materials for franken prototyping should be easy to handle and inexpensive.

Inspiration Collage

I created an inspiration collage to express materials, scenes when and where the product is going to be used, places where the product is going to be sold, and lifestyles around the product.

Scenario

I drew a short scenario to show the scene in which the product is going to be used. In this scenario, a user connects the two ends of the band by a snap and put two pens on the holder, assembling with the notebook he/she has already had. The reason why the band has two ends is that a user can choose a penholder part and a band independently.
